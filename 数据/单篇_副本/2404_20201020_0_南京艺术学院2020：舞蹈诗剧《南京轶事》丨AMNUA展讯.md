**第二幕   金陵记忆**_MEMORIES OF NANJING__SCENE 2__古都南京历经朝代更替战火纷飞，在风云聚散中接受洗礼，流民迁徙中脱胎换骨。洋务运动打开了中国封建教育制度的缺口，金陵制造局等军事工业的创办，拉开了中国工业走向近代化的序幕。民国早期看似和平的年代却暗藏汹涌，眼前的美好稍纵即逝。战争的爆发，致使南京城内断壁残垣、满目疮痍。_

_Nanjing, the ancient capital of China,__experienced the change of dynasties and__eras of wars. The westernization movement__leads the gap in China's feudal education__system, and the establishment of military__industries such as Nanjing manufacturing__bureau which paved the prelude of the__modernization of China's industry. In the__early phase of the republic of China, the__seemingly peaceful era was turbulent and__the present beauty was fleeting. During the__outbreak of the war, the walls of the City of__Nanjing were left in ruins._

**第三幕南京符号**_ANTIQUITIES OF SIX DYNASTIES__SCENE 3THE SYMBOLS OF NANJING__幸存者是历史的证人，证人老去，记忆永存，陪伴他们的是落寞、是毁于一旦的家园、是永远无法弥补的伤痛。一衣带水长江千载，见证南京的桑田沧海。南京长江大桥的建成，成为了沟通南北交通的大 动脉。千年古都在岁月长河中迈向新时代，南京明城墙、南京大牌档、老门东、盐水鸭成为南京新的时尚符号。新时代的南京人在饱经沧桑的故土上劳作生息，年轻一代肩负着历史赋予的责任与使命，城市建设者日夜劳作，保障了城市的万千霓虹。在文都的土地上，人们各司 其职，于桨声灯影里，共同描绘新时代的南京。_

_Survivors are the “witnesses” of history,__while the witnesses grow old, their memory__stays eterna. Their loneliness for their__once called home was destroyed and no__matter how much repair is done, the pain__can never be repaired. A strip of water that__for thousands of years, witness the great__changes of Nanjing. The completion of the__Nanjing Yangtze river bridge has become__a major artery which connect the north to__the south provinces. The ancient capital of__a thousand years is moving towards a new__era in the long river of time. Nanjing Ming__city wall, "Nanjing Impressions Restaurant",__Lao Mendong district , and the salted__ducks have become the new symbols of__fashion in Nanjing. In the new era, the__people of Nanjing live and work on the old__land, and the young generation undertake__the responsibility and mission which was__crafted by history. The builders of the city__work day in and night out to ensure the__thousands of neon lights are all brighten__up in the city. In the city, people perform__their duties. Under the myriad sound and__light shades people all come together to__illustrate the New Age of Nanjing._!!
