策划： **WUXU** 研究项目（意大利 & 中国）

五十年前在意大利，在全球的社会运动浪潮中，影像生产从塑造景观的工具成为瓦解它的武器：一群充满勇气的文化艺术工作者们冲击了代表权力的话语，创造出新的语言；同时知识分子与工人聚集到一起——“意大利工人主义：令人惊异的革命思想”也在这里诞生了。

时下技术对身体与自然的塑造，城镇与乡村在不同文化间更广泛的连接，让我们正经历着一个景观快速更迭的时代。由此我们大胆预想在不远的将来，我们会迎来一种新形态的社会与自由。而任何的变革与社会运动都有它的独一的属性。在意大利的六八运动历史中，我们尝试捕获其中的当代精神与它的独一性，由此更敏锐地维持思想与行动的独立。

**WUXU**

**In Italy fifty years ago, amidst a global tide of social movements, film transformed the tools that made spectacles into weapons that would destroy them. A group of courageous intellectuals and artists attacked discourses that represented power to create a new language. Intellectuals and workers gathered together and Italian Workerism was born.**

**As current technologies shape bodies and nature, there are broad links between cities and villages in different cultures, which has allowed us to experience an era in which spectacles have changed rapidly. As a result, we boldly envision that, in the not-too- distant future, we will welcome a new form of society and freedom. All revolutions and social movements have their unique properties. We attempted to capture the contemporaneity and singularity of Italy’s Movement ’68, while keenly maintaining independence of thought and action.**

**WUXU**

关于 **WUXU About WUXU/**

WUXU是于2017年在博洛尼亚建立的非盈利研究机构。WUXU关注意大利的社会现场，欧洲与东亚间文化交流，跨学科研究与当代艺术实践。WUXU旨在建立一个研究者的联合体，它将连结大众（Multitude），并独立地行动。同时也通过机构运营本身，来明晰社会运转的机制。

务虚的释义分为两个部分：

一是关于讨论本质规律（to discuss principles）；二是对“务实”不同的审视。

务虚也是史铁生的《务虚笔记》，它连结个人的历史与时代变革的进程，并以思想去叙事。

WUXU is a research no – profit association founded in Bologna in 2017. WUXU focuses on the Italian social reality, Europe – Far East cultural exchanges and interdisciplinary and practical research on contemporary arts. WUXU aims to build a researchers team which is well connected with the multitude and at the same time can keep an independent way to proceed. At the same time, through its work, the organization aims to establish its role in the social mechanism.

The term WUXU has two meanings:

The first one means “theoretical discussion”, the second one regards the theoretical approach to a practical issue, or wushi. The term wuxu was also used by Chinese author ShiTiesheng in his Notes on Principles (Wuxu Biji), a  book which collects a set of personal views of the writer on social development issues.
