**_前言_**

“大日月”是艺术家张明信的网名。

2021年11月开始，大日月活跃于抖音、快手等自媒体平台，以一位寓教于乐的美术老师的人设形象，做直播，录制教学课程，制作短视频，教绘画零基础的粉丝画画。

白手起家，从零开始，大日月的粉丝与日俱增，他创作的短视频迄今已有上千万的曝光量，已经达到月收入人民币40000+的水平。

在做直播、录制课程和创作短视频的过程中，大日月创作了无数张小尺幅的绘画作品，材料以纸本马克笔、纸本油画棒为多，还有少量丙烯画作品。

时代在发展，大众粉丝的生活质量在提升，美学需求在增大，每个扎进时代、潜入生活的人物都有美好的未来。

大日月的艺术创作任重道远，他的美术教育事业才刚刚开始。

—— 何迟

‘大日月' (Big Sun and Moon) is the username of the artist Zhang Mingxin. Starting in November 2021, Big Sun and Moon became active on self-media platforms like Douyin and Kuaishou. He adopted the persona of an art teacher who educates through entertainment. He conducted live broadcasts, recorded instructional courses, created short videos, and taught his fans to draw from scratch.

Starting from scratch, Big Sun and Moon's fan base grew steadily. The short videos he created have now garnered exposure to millions, and his monthly income has reached over 40,000 RMB (Chinese Yuan).

During the process of live streaming, course recording, and creating short videos, Big Sun and Moon produced numerous small-scale artworks, primarily using paper and markers, paper and oil pastels, and a few acrylic paintings.

As the era evolves, the quality of life for the general public is improving, and there is a growing demand for aesthetics. Every individual who immerses themselves in the era and delves into life has a promising future.

Big Sun and Moon's artistic journey is a long and challenging one, and his career in art education is just beginning.

By He Chi

**_艺术家简介_**

张明信，1985年生于山东滕州，2008年毕业于湖北美术学院版画系，现工作生活于北京。

张明信是把艺术混入社会，把社会混入生活，把生活混入艺术的艺术家。艺术是挑战。要挑战社会，挑战观众，先挑战生活，挑战自己。自己既是自己挑战的对象，也是挑战自己的方法。他的艺术行动也常常使观众遇到了挑战：哪里是艺术？或者艺术在哪里？如果对张明信来说，挑战即混淆，那么对观众来说，挑战即辨别。

**About Zhang Mingxin**

Zhang Mingxin was born in Tengzhou, Shandong in 1985 and graduated from the Hubei Institute of Fine Arts in 2008. He currently lives and works in Beijing.

Zhang is an artist who mixes art with social life, social life with personal life, and life with art.

Art is a challenge. To challenge society and the audience, he first challenges his own life and himself. The self is both the target and the method of the challenge. Also, his artistic actions often challenge the audience: Can these actions be considered art? Or which part is art? If for Zhang Mingxin, to challenge is to create confusion, then the challenge for the audience is to make distinctions.

Selected Solo Projects: Dream 5 Million' Jinze Homestead, Beijing, 2015；Five-Star Hotel Action Art Space, Beijing, 2014-2015.A selection of major art practices: Co-founded 'Mobile Art Museum' in 2017 (has withdrawn).A selection of major group exhibitions: Media Art×Fair' at U.Square, Gwangju, South Korea, 2019; 'Exit - Chinese Experimental Short Film and Video Exhibition' at Nanjing Art Institute Art Museum, Nanjing, 2016; “Unlived by What is Seen” Tang Contemporary Art, Pace Beijing, Galleria Continua, Beijing,2014; Harmony in Diversity' at 14+14 Contemporary Art Invitation Exhibition, Beijing Contemporary Art Museum, Beijing, 2012; The 3rd 1912 New Star Art Festival' at Sanchuan Contemporary Art Museum, Nanjing, 2012; China Narratives' in the United States, New York, 2010; International Animation Art Festival - China POP ART' at Gallery Fleu, Seoul, South Korea, 2010; 'Build White' and '1+Up Art Scene' from Liangdao Street, Wuchang, to Hankou Riverside, Wuhan, 2005.
