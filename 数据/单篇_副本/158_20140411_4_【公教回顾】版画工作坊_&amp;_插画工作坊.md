【COA艺术跨界】项目介绍：

南京艺术学院美术馆自2014年3月起，将全面开启“CrossOver Art”（COA）艺术跨界公共教育项目。此项目以南京艺术学院作为综合类艺术院校为背景依托，整合校内各学院艺术专业的优势资源，开发集版画、插画、舞蹈、器乐、话剧等多重形式为一体的多元跨界艺术项目，并定期举行成果展示、小型演出。

Since march 2014,AMNUA will vitalize "CrossOver Art"(COA), which is a cross-border art public education project.Nanjing University of the Arts(NUA) is a comprehensive arts institutions. Relying on this background, this project integrates professional resources of each college of NUA and develops multiple forms of the diverse cross-border art project, such as printing,illustration,dancing,instrumental music,drama and so forth. Also, AMNUA will hold achievement shows and small performances regularly.
