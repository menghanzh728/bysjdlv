缪晓春

1989年毕业于中央美术学院，获硕士学位；1999年毕业于德国卡塞尔美术学院，获硕士学位。现为中央美术学院教授。

缪晓春90年代开始在现实与虚拟的界面探索和创作，在摄影、绘画和三维电脑动画等多个领域并行工作。其极富现代感的散点透视摄影作品体现出对历史与现世开拓性的联结。他用3D技术将二维绘画转换为三维动画，在改变了传统的看画方式的同时，更以其对历史和未来的想象给艺术史上的经典之作带来全新的诠释及意义，为当代与艺术史的融合提供了又一个重要范例。对新技术多种可能性的持续探索使其自2009年起发展了一系列新的绘画--“算法绘画”，并在2013年威尼斯双年展中国馆与新的三维电脑动画作品同时展出。

近期举办的个展包括：“缪晓春：遣词造句，上海阿拉里奥画廊”（上海，2015）；“缪晓春：另存为”，北京白盒子艺术馆（北京798, 2015）；“缪晓春：两个大录像”（今日美术馆，北京，2010）；“缪晓春-宏观狂”（德国路德维希博物馆，科布伦茨，2010）。群展包括“第55届威尼斯双年展”（威尼斯，中国馆，2013）；首届北京国际摄影双年展（北京，2013）；“第7届亚太当代艺术三年展电影回顾展”（布里斯本，2012）；“首届基辅当代艺术双年展”（基辅，2012）；“第4届广州三年展”（广州，2012/2011）；“成都双年展”（2011/2005）；“广州国际摄影双年展”（广州，2009/2005）；“釜山双年展”（釜山，2008）；“韩国首尔国际媒体艺术双年展”（首尔，2006/2002）；“第4届上海双年展”（上海，2002）等。

作品为纽约现代美术馆、波士顿美术馆、芝加哥斯玛特艺术博物馆、维也纳路德维希现代艺术基金会博物馆、法国国家当代艺术基金会、新加坡美术馆、上海美术馆等收藏。

Miao Xiaochun

Miao Xiaochun graduated from the China Central Academy ofFine Arts (CAFA) with MA Degree in 1989 and from the Kunsthochschule Kassel,Germany with MA Degree in 1999. Professor at CAFA.

He started in 90s his creativeexplorations on the interface between the real and the virtual. His extensivebody of work includes photography, painting and 3D computer animation which areparallel to each other.

He uses 3D technology to createupon a 2D image a virtual 3D scene, to transform a still canvas into movingimages, concurrently changing the traditional way of viewing paintings andgiving a completely new interpretation and significance to a masterpiece ofart, especially with his imagination about history and the future. His worksadd an important example to contemporary negotiations with art history.

With his constant exploration ofmultiple possibilities of new technology, he has developed since 2009 a seriesof Algorithmic Paintings exhibitedtogether with his new 3D computer animations in the Chinese Pavilion duringVenice Biennale 2013.

His recent solo exhibitions include: “MiaoXiaochun:Grammar”, Arario Shanghai(Shanghai,2015); “MiaoXiaochun:Save As”,Whitebox Art Center“(Beijing 798, 2015); “Miao Xiaochun-Two Big Video Works”, TodayArt Museum (Beijing, 2010) ; “Miao Xiaochun-Macromania”, Ludwig Museum (Koblenz,Germany, 2010). He participated in many international biennales such as The55th Venice Biennale（Venice, Chinese Pavilion, 2013）;The 1st Beijing Photo Biennial (Beijing, 2013); The 7th Asia-PacificTriennial of Contemporary Art- Chinese Animation Since the 1930s（Brisbane，2012）;The 1st Kyiv International Biennial of Contemporary Art（Kyiv，2012）;The 4th Guangzhou Triennial（Guangzhou，2012/2011）;Chengdu Biennale（2011/2005）; Guangzhou PhotoBiennial（Guangzhou，2009/2005）;The Seoul International Media Art Biennale（2006/2002）;The 4th Shanghai Biennale（Shanghai，2002）,etc.

Major museum collections include: MoMA New York;TheMuseum of Fine Arts, Boston; The Smart Museum of Art, Chicago; Museum ModernerKunst Stiftung Ludwig, Vienna; Fondation Nationale d’Art Contemporain(FNAC),Paris; Singapore Art Museum；Shanghai Art Museum,etc.
