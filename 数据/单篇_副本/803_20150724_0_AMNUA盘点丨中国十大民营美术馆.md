#AMNUA盘点丨中国十大民营美术馆

在中国，越来越多的当代美术馆以一种前所未有的速度拔地而起，作为运营者的私企或个人不仅应该了解如何建起一座美术馆，往往更应该懂得如何以一种高于中国公共机构的水准去对其进行运作。基于对展览、活动项目及建筑等诸多因素的考量，本文罗列了十座中国最好的民营美术馆（排名不分先后）。

China is building contemporary art museums at an unrivalled pace. Private companies and individuals increasingly have not only the means to establish these museums but also the know-how to keep them going, often to a higher standard than China’s public institutions. This report lists ten of the best private art museums in China (in no particular order), based on everything from their exhibitions and events programs to their architecture.

!

喜马拉雅美术馆 Himalayas Art Museum上海Shanghai

喜马拉雅美术馆前身为创建于2005年的上海证大现代艺术馆。2012年迁入现址，一座由建筑师矶崎新设计的拥有洞穴般墙面的穴状空间。不论从展览频率或主题来说，喜马拉雅美术馆都稍显不够规律，但它的确举办过不少出色的展览，比如托尼·克拉格、迈克马丁、荒木经惟等艺术家的个展以及一个给予了艺术家唐茂宏很大发挥空间的新媒体艺术展。

The Himalayas Art Museum (as it is better known, but never call it HAM) began in 2005 as the Shanghai Zendai MoMa. It moved to its current location in 2012, a cavernous space—with truly cave-like walls—designed by Arata Isozaki. Exhibitions are a little irregular with regards to both frequency and subject matter, but it has hosted some excellent shows, including solos by Tony Cragg, Michael Craig-Martin and Noboyushi Araki, and a contemporary new media show that gave a great platform to Tang Maohong.

!

今日美术馆 Today Art Museum北京 Beijing

作为中国第一家非营利性质的民营美术馆，今日美术馆由张宝全先生创立于2002年。主馆占地4000平米，坐落于首都的朝阳区CBD中心，由建筑师王晖设计。其展览注意力主要集中于中国当代艺术，曾为刘小东、刘炜、冯梦波等许多重量级艺术家举办过个展。

Founded by Zhang Baoquan in 2002, the Today Art Museum was the first not-for-profit private museum in China. Located in Chaoyang District, in the capital’s CBD, the 4,000 square metre complex was designed by Wang Hui. and shows focus almost exclusively on Chinese contemporary art. Past solo shows have been dedicated to artists such by Liu Xiaodong, Liu Wei, Feng Mengbo, and other such art heavyweights.

!

四方当代美术馆 Sifang Art Museum南京 Nanjing

四方当代美术馆坐落于南京市郊四方艺术湖区的入口处。四方艺术湖区正式名称为“中国国际建筑艺术实践展”，邀请了David Adjaye，王澍，SANNA，艾未未，Mathiaz Klotz等超过20位国际顶尖设计师和艺术家共同参与这一项目。每位受邀者所设计的原创住宅将作艺术家驻留或度假休憩之用。四方美术馆建筑本身为 StevenHoll建筑团队设计，可以说是世界上最酷的美术馆建筑之一。馆内所展艺术家包括Danh Vo，段建宇，Olafur Eliasson，毛焰，徐震，杨福东，张培力和廖国核等。

The Sifang is located outside Nanjing at the gateway of Sifang Parkland, formerly known as the Contemporary International Practical Exhibition of Architecture. At the site, over 20 award-winning artists and architects—including David Adjaye, Wang Shu, Ai Weiwei and Mathias Klotz—were invited to construct original homes to serve as artists’ residencies and vacation destinations. The Sifang itself was designed by Steven Holl Architects and is one of the most handsome museums anywhere. Artists to show there include Danh Vo, Duan Jianyu, Olafur Eliasson, Mao Yan, Xu Zhen, Yang Fudong, Zhang Peili, and Liao Guohe.

!

华侨城当代艺术中心 OCT ContemporaryArt Terminal深圳，上海，西安 Shenzhen, Shanghai and Xi’an

2005年，华侨城当代艺术中心（或称OCAT）建立于深圳华侨城（如今已被中国国家旅游局评为5A级旅游景区）。随后，由张培力担任执行馆长的OCAT上海馆及由Karen Smith掌管的OCAT西安馆也相继上线。OCAT现已成为发展最为蓬勃的新媒体艺术平台之一。

The OCT Contemporary Art Terminal (or OCAT, to stack acronym on acronym), was established in Shenzhen’s Overseas Chinese Town, what the China National Tourism Administration calls a AAAAA scenic area, in 2005. Since then, OCAT has set up shop in Shanghai, where it’s helmed by Zhang Peili, and in Xi’an, where its led by Karen Smith. OCAT is one of the most progressive platforms for new media art.

!

时代美术馆 Times Museum广州 Guangzhou

在充满不寻常美术馆的中国，时代美术馆可以说是最耐人寻味的那种。其馆体建筑的外骨架由雷姆·库哈斯设计，被附在了一所位于广州市中心与机场之间的住宅内部。时代美术馆的展览不仅致力于为国内外当代艺术家提供平台（PipilottiRist是最近的一个亮点），还注重与家住楼下或相邻街区的本土社区居民展开互动。

In a country full of unusual art museums, the Times is one of the most intriguing. The museum is an architectural exoskeleton—designed by Rem Koolhass, no less—affixed to an apartment block midway between downtown Guangzhou and the airport. Exhibitions aim not only to provide a platform for Chinese and international contemporary artists (Pipilotti Rist is a recent highlight) but also to engage the very local community: the residents living downstairs and in the surrounding apartment blocks.

!

余德耀美术馆 Yuz Museum上海 Shanghai

印尼华人藏家余德耀对大型作品的迷恋促使他选用了一座废弃的机场飞机库作为展览空间。所展作品中的重点包括徐冰用香烟描绘的虎皮地毯，孙原、彭禹的《自由》（一根极高水压作用下的消防水管在经历了长时间的平静后突然爆发）等。今年早前恰值开馆周年之际，余德耀美术馆推出了自己的第二个展览《天人之际II》，即开幕展《天人之际》的延续。今年9月1日至12月1日，余德耀美术馆还将会展出首次在亚洲露面的兰登国际作品《雨屋》。

Chinese-Indonesian collector Budi Tek is such a fan of “mega-works” that he needed a disused aircraft hangar to show his impressive collection. Among the highlights are Xu Bing’s cigarette tiger pelt, Peng Yu and Sun Yuan’s Freedom, a high pressure fire hose that, after long intermissions of calm, furiously lashes out. The museum celebrated its first birthday earlier this year with the launch of its second show, a sequel to the inaugural exhibition, Myth History. From 1 September to 1 December, 2015 the Yuz will be showing Rain Room (2012) by Random International for the first time in Asia.

!

红砖美术馆 Red Brick Art Museum北京 Beijing

2013年，红砖美术馆曾被《经济学人》杂志拿来当作中国美术馆中展览难以与美术馆建筑相匹配的案例。的确，红砖美术馆地理位置偏远（位于北京五环路以外的何各庄村），但自那时起它就迅速确立了作为严肃艺术机构的定位。这座由藏家闫士杰创立的美术馆采用了中国传统园林意象来精心打造。直至今年的9月25日，观众都能来此看到正在举行的Olafur Eliasson作品展《水钟摆》。

In 2013, Red Brick Art Museum was held up by The Economist as an example of China’s museum-building outstripping its capacity to create quality exhibitions. Red Brick is out of the way (beyond Beijing’s fifth ring road in the Hegezhuang village) but since then, it has quickly established itself as a serious institution. Opened by Yan Zhijie, the museum is built on sculpted Chinese gardens. It is showing Water Pendulum by Olafur Eliasson through 25 September 2015.

!

龙美术馆（西岸馆）Long Museum (West Bund)上海 Shanghai

开馆第一年，龙美术馆因作为王薇、刘益谦夫妇海量藏品的库房而闻名。如今，它开始展示一批优异的原创作品，其中徐震、丁乙的个展已成为中国艺术年历的重要亮点。除去这些最新的当代艺术委托项目以外，龙美术馆的地下层还藏有大量传统水墨画及现代中国油画。

What began, in its first year, as a glorified warehouse for Wang Wei and Liu Yiqian’s considerable collection has now begun showing some incredible original work. Solo shows by Xu Zhen and Ding Yi have been major highlights of the Chinese art calendar this year. In addition to exciting new contemporary art commissions, the Long has a large collection of traditional ink paintings and modern Chinese oil paintings on its subterranean floors.

!

外滩美术馆 Rockbund Art Museum上海 Shanghai

外滩美术馆从开馆初期就为国内知名艺术家举办了一系列重量级个展。蔡国强为“农民达芬奇”的发明组织了展览，曾梵志在他的乱草画作之间搭起了一对巨大的象牙，张桓用机械制作的孔子吓惨了一群活蹦乱跳的猴子。自LarysFrogier接任馆长以来，一反国内主要关注本土当代艺术家及国外昔日大师的风潮，为外滩带来了Paola Pivi,Bharti Kher及Ugo Rondinone等一批国际当代艺术家的展览。如今，已经开馆5年的外滩美术馆拥有全国最好的公共活动和教育项目，并推动设立了振奋人心的Hugo Boss亚洲艺术大奖。

Rockbund Art Museum (RAM) opened with a series of blockbuster solo shows by star Chinese artists. Cai Guo-Qiang arranged a show of found inventions by “peasant da Vincis”, Zeng Fanzhi erected a huge pair of mammoth tusks among his bracken-filled paintings, and Zhang Huan created an animatronic Confucius with which to scare live monkeys. Since Larys Frogier took over as director, shows by Paola Pivi, Bharti Kher and Ugo Rondinone have brought a welcome injection of international contemporary artists to a part of the world that tends to focus more on local contemporary artists and foreign past masters. Now five years old, RAM has one of the best events and education programs in China, and has helped launch the exciting new Hugo Boss Asia Art Prize.

!

尤伦斯当代艺术中心 The UllensCentre for Contemporary Art北京 Beijing

尤伦斯当代艺术中心于2007年由比利时藏家尤伦斯夫妇创立。作为中国展览制造的先锋，它不仅为许多国内知名艺术家举办个展，还支持了一批国内最重要的策展项目，比如2013年鲍栋和孙冬冬的标志性展览“ON/OFF”。尤伦斯当代艺术中心的艺术商店作为全国最好的艺术设计类纪念品商店，为798浓厚的当代艺术商业倾向提供了重要的语境平衡。

Founded by Belgian collectors Guy and Miriam Ullens in 2007, the Ullens Centre for Contemporary Art (UCCA) is at the forefront of exhibition making in China. As well as solo shows for many of China’s leading artists, it has paved the way for some of the most important curatorial projects, including Bao Dong and Sun Dongdong’s iconic ON/OFF exhibition in 2013. At the same time as having the country’s best art/design gift shop, UCCA provides important contextual counterbalance to the more commercial contemporary art leanings of Beijing’s 798.
