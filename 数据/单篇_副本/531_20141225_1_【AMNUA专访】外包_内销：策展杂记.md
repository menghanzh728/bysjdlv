王亚敏WangYamin 16:15

In global producing system, body labour is still significant , hands knocking the keyboards, minds curating the exhibitions , space built to show art, hard disks with wireless cables produced and set up to expand the art in internet and artists showing their bodys and living lives of performers, etc. so, what is the ghost in body? Here, it is the global capitalism now acting as freedom-sm by the mask of new internet era? And , we still wish the ghost in shell is the “ I” with her or his property. But now, they finally are exploited to death. We are surviving the re-Proletarianized progress again and again now. The body is still being re-Proletarianized, which will attract shakes with her smell. In fact, although the contemporary art is totally a shell, ghost in a shell will still be exploited to death./在全球的制造系统中，身体劳动仍然占据着重要地位：双手敲击着键盘；头脑策划着展览；建造了展示艺术的空间；生产出了硬盘然后在互联网上扩散艺术；艺术家展示自己的身体，以度过表演的一生，等等。所以，寄居于身体中的“灵”是什么呢？当下，它是戴着新互联网时代面具的全球资本主义，装扮成了自由主义？甚至，我们还梦想这个躯壳中的灵，她就是那个有她或者他的属性的“我”。但现在，终究被剥削至死。我们如今在反反复复地经受“再无产阶级化”进程。身体依然被再无产化，身体的味道只要一点点足以吸引鲨鱼。实际上，整个当代艺术虽然是壳/shell，ghost in shell /攻壳依然难逃厄运。
