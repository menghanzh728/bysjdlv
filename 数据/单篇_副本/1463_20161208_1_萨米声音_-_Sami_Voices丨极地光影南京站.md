**萨米声音 IV. SAMI VOICES**

萨米人是北欧的原住民，他们居住的领域覆盖斯堪的纳维亚半岛北部的大部分地区。萨米人以饲养驯鹿为生，也从事狩猎和渔业。今天，生活在瑞典、挪威、芬兰的萨米人基本都已融进了现代社会的生活，但依旧很大程度地保持着自己的语言和文化，新一代的萨米艺术家通过音乐、电影来表达的正是在这种传统与现代的交织中对自己身份来源的思考，对历史遗留的伤疤的反思。影片中有记录重要的萨米音乐家MariBoine以音乐为媒介将新老文化完美结合的成功尝试，和年轻女音乐人SofiaJannok用萨米人特殊的原生态歌喉对现代生活的表达。

The Sámiare the indigenous people of Scandinavia, traditionally associated with anomadic way of life and Reindeer herding. They have also been living in coastal areas, making livelihoods by both land and sea. Today Sámi tradition and language live on in both Finland, Norway and Sweden, but in negotiation with modern life. The films in this program are by young filmmakers, offering both a look back at a history of repression, but also at different Sámi identities of today. Great musicians, among them the eminent Mari Boine, demonstrate how old and new canbe fruitfully combined.

**影片／Films**

**_萨米男孩_** **_Sami Boy_**

剧情片Fiction\|2015\|彩色Color\|9min\|萨米地区Sápmi

!

Mikkel是一名驯鹿猎人，家里围猎驯鹿的活靠地全是他。和所有的萨米男孩一样，他有一个坚强的外表，但他的内心却是一团糟。

Mikkel is a reindeer herder and has the entire responsibility for the herd of his family. He has a tough shell like a sámi bojá should have. But in his inside there is chaos.

**导演简介 Director's Bio**

_艾丽·索芙·亨里克森 Elle Sofe Henriksen_

_!_

艾丽·索芙·亨里克森1984年生于挪威的北极村凯于图凯努，她在舞蹈、编舞和电影方面均有造诣。艾丽从一个萨米人的身份出发，通过舞蹈和电影，将萨米人的情感世界呈献给更广泛的观众。她拥有伦敦雷班学校的舞蹈学士学位和奥斯陆国家艺术学院舞蹈专业的硕士学位。《萨米男孩》获得Skårungen奖提名。

Elle Sofe Henriksen, born 1984, is from Guovdageaidnu/Kautokeino, Norway. Elle Sofe works with dance, choreography and film. Elle is engaged in presenting Sami expressions from an insider's perspective to a wider audience through dance and film. She has a Masters degree in choreography from Oslo National Academy of the Arts and BA in Dance at the Laban School in London.

**_少女麦根_** **_O.M.G (OH, MA_** **_́_** **_IGONGIRL)_**

剧情片Fiction\|2015\|彩色Color\|19min\|萨米地区Sápmi

!

在北极苔藓地漫长光亮的白昼里，两个少女厌烦了村庄里的生活，她们想做一些冒险。天真的少女麦根被比她更有经验的好朋友挑战而感到压力，可麦根也在想念住在更远地方的完美男孩。为了见他，两个少女踏上了改变命运的搭车旅行。

Two teenage girls are bored of their village. They want some adventure, the naive Máigon is challenged by her more experienced best friend to debut and she feels the pressure. Meanwhile Máigon dreams of the perfect boy, but he lives far away and it leads them on a fateful hitchhiking trip.

**导演简介 Director's Bio**

_玛丽亚·博·楠戈 Marja Bål Nango_

_!_

玛丽亚·博·楠戈（1988年生）是一名来自挪威北方Galgujávri的萨米艺术家和电影人，她先后在诺德兰艺术电影学院，利勒哈默尔大学学院电影和电视研究专业和萨米大学学院电影制片专业学习。2010年当选挪威Riddu Riddu原住民艺术节年度青年艺术家，在艺术节上做个人影展。2012年创作的儿童短片《圣诞精灵》被挪威电影局购买在电影院向全挪威儿童放映。目前，她在制作一部纪录片和完成一个电影剧本。

Marja Bål Nango (b.1988) is an Sámi artist and filmmaker from Galgujávri in Norway. Educated at the Nordland College of Art, Film and Film & Television studies in Lillehammer University College and film producing at Sámi University College. During  Riddu Riddu Indigenous Festival 2011, she was the young artist of the year, whereshe had an solo-exhibition of art and her own film program. She has screened her films at a various of film festivals. Her short film for children, _Juletrollet_ ( _The Christmas Troll)_ was bought by The Norwegian Film Institute and broadcasted through a short film program for children at cinemas all over Norway in 2012. She is now working on two different film projects with a three year film grant, a documentary project and a longer film script.

**_燃烧的太阳_** **_Burning Sun_**

剧情片Fiction\|2015\|彩色Color\|13min\|萨米地区Sápmi

!

一个年轻的萨米族女孩儿Majjen发现传教士们出现在了村子里，他们开始焚烧萨米女孩的帽子。惊慌中Majjen躲藏起来，但还是被教堂的人发现了。她试图逃跑，最后演变成了一场激烈的争斗。Majjen要留住她祖上留给她的帽子，传教士却说帽子的顶部住着魔鬼。

A young sámi-girl, Majjen, discover church gentlemen who have arrived in the village to burn their female hats. She hides in fear but is detected by the church gentlemen. She tries to flee and it turns into an intense battle. Majjen wants to keep the hat which she has inherited. Church Lords is convinced that at the tip of the hat sits the devil.

**导演简介 Director's Bio**

_艾丽·玛丽亚·艾勒 Elle Márjá Eira_

_!_

艾丽·玛丽亚是一名来自北极的Kaitokeino的萨米作家、音乐家、电影制片人和驯鹿猎人。她拥有挪威芬马克大学学院媒体制片专业学位。艾丽·玛丽亚为挪威广播公司MánáidTV的数部连续剧做过导演和编剧。她为自己乐队导演的音乐短片《Guođohit狩猎》在2013年的特罗姆瑟国际电影节举行了首映。她同时也是《萨米男孩》的制片人。

_Elle Márjá Eira_ is a Sami writer, film producer, singer and reindeer hunter from Kautokeino. She has degree in Media production from Finnmark University College. Elle Márjáhas directed and written screenplays for several NRK Mánáid TV series. Together with Ken Are Bongo, she directed a music video "Guođohit - To herd" for her own band, which premiered at the Tromsø International Film Festival 2013. She is also the producer of Sami Boy.

**_拉普兰德联合_** **_S_** **_á_** **_pmi Sessions_**

纪录片Documentary\|2014\|彩色Color\|28min\|瑞典Sweden

!

《拉普兰德联合》是一部讲诉两位世界著名的音乐人：萨米音乐家和瑞典作曲家共同创作一首歌曲的纪录片。在这里，一部此生绝无仅有的特别的歌曲创作跨越了音乐、文化和语言的界限。两位音乐家刚刚从卖座的环球巡演和紧张的演出行程中抽出时间，旅行了数百公里，在Vássejávri火车站外第一次见面。这是一个位于瑞典最北端的废弃萨米村庄，群山环绕，万籁俱寂，两位艺术家们开始了改变她们一生的创作。从开始有些尴尬的沉默，到音乐家的自我怀疑，失缺灵感，情感的过山车营造魔幻时刻，泪水和欢笑，一首新歌终于诞生。

SÁPMI SESSIONS is a documentary series about how Sápmi's most celebrated artist and Sweden's greatest artist came together. Crossing the borders of musical genres, culture and language, this is a once-in-a-lifetime session where they create one new song together. Coming straight from sold-out world tours and packed schedules, they travel hundreds of miles and meet for the first time in Sápmi. Three days of tears, laughter and magic, and hopefully a new song. Three days, two artists, one new song.

Lisa Marie Kristensen quit college to make the first season of SÁPMI SESSIONS back in 2010, and has been with Sveriges Television since then. She is from Kautokeino but now lives many different places.

**_如海般广阔_** **_Á_** **_HPI - WIDE AS OCEANS_**

音乐录影带MTV\|2013\|彩色Color\|4min\|萨米地区Sápmi

!

萨米歌手索菲亚的歌声将传统的萨米吟唱同现代电子音乐自由结合，成为代表北欧声音的世界音乐的著名音乐人。她的歌曲描绘她生活的萨米土地和这里年轻人与现代社会的冲突。

Sofia Jannok is a Sami singer. Her songs combine the traditional Sami joiking with modern electric music win her a well claimed name within world music genre. She sings about the Sápmi land where she comes from and the young people's life and their conflicts  with the modern society.

Oskar is a Sámi director that has worked with films in many different genres for more than 10 years. Co-owner of the production company _Baut_ _a_ _film_ in Northern Sweden.
