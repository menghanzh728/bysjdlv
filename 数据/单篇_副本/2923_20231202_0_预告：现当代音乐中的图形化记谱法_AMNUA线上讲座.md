_**讲座内容**_

《讯息即媒介》第二季第6集《余音》除了展览整体传达层面上的，关于艺术代际及其思维和生产模式的“混淆视听”，也非常具体地落实在艺术家的一系列研究和制作上，包括对像声音和图形这二类根本上异质对立媒介的进一步混淆。 **“图形化记谱”** 是其中的一个重要操作方程式之一。对比当代艺术中围绕视觉和声音的对位转换的操作，《现当代音乐中的图形化记谱法》演讲者则主要在现当代音乐的语境中来考察类似操作。

_The confusion in audio-visual perception in Episode 6, "Resonance," of the second season of "The Message is Medium" is not only manifested at the overall level of conveying intergenerational aspects of art and its modes of thinking and production but is also concretely implemented in the artist's research and production processes. This includes a further confusion in audio-visual perception, particularly in the fundamental heterogeneity and opposition of media such as sound and graphics. "Graphic Notation" is one of the important operational formulas in this context. In contrast to the operations surrounding the visual and auditory positional transformations in contemporary art, the speaker in “Graphic and Extended Notation in Contemporary Music_ _” primarily examines similar operations in the context of modern and contemporary music._

_**主讲人简介**_

**栾复祥**，乐评人，长期从事现当代音乐的介绍、分析与推广工作，特别关注法国频谱音乐、罗马尼亚当代，音乐与伊阿尼斯·泽纳基斯的研究。曾供稿于《三联爱乐》《音乐爱好者》《国家大剧院》《音乐周报》以及北京国际音乐节等。创办当代音乐自媒体“新音乐手册”，文章得到读者的一定认可。近年来从事音乐家采访、当代音乐活动的策划以及展出声音设计等工作，曾策划上海当代艺术博物馆泽纳基斯百年系列活动、上海当代艺术博物馆声音装置“思槽”，济南怪物房独立艺术展等。曾合作艺术家包括梁雷、杜韵、程慧惠、秦文琛、依克山·阿卜都沙拉木、李凝、藤仓大（Dai Fujikura）、川岛素晴（Motoharu Kawashima）、博齐基纳（Olga Bochikhina）等。

_Fuxiang LUAN, a music reviewer and freelance musicologist who is self-devoted into the introduction, promotion, and analysis of contemporary music especially on the study of spectralism, Romanian contemporary music and the composer Iannis Xenakis. Articles has been published by Sanlian Philharmonic, The Music Lovers, NCPA classics, music weekly and Beijing International Music Festival.etc In recent years, Luan has been engaged in multiple fields including interviews, contemporary concerts, installations. Past projects including the Xenakis 100 anniversary projects of PSA, spatialized music installation «Thinking Sink » , and The Monster House exhibition in Jinan. Luan has collaborated with Lei Liang, Yun Du, Huihui Cheng, Wenchen Qin, Yikeshan Abudushalamu, Ning Li, Dai Fujikura, Motoharu Kawashima, Olga Bochikhina.etc_
