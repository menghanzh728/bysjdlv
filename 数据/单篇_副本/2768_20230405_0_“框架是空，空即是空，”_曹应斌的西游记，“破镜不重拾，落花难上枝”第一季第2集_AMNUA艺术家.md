作为《讯息即媒介》的第2集，《西游记，曹应斌》似乎要将我们完全带入并沉浸于传统媒介——绘画的世界中。我们在这里试图挑选一些可以帮助我们暂时逃离这里的点，转换我们观看和思考的框架。

‘第5展厅是一个相对封闭、干净的空间，适合展示一些小型绘画作品’，曹老师认为这个展厅非常“聚气”。与一般的白盒子空间不同，它位于AMNUA美术馆建筑螺旋体尾巴的偏狭顶层中，是一个绵延的洞穴一样的半坡顶空间。

!

展览现场 exhibition view

似乎一切都是和表示可移动性的“架上”相对立，这批绘画在某种程度上被认为天然属于这个空间和场地。这就让我们想起：以西方绘画史来看，在成为“架上”之前，绘画其实在很大程度上是依附于建筑和空间的，不可移动的“壁上”绘画。最典型的就是这次展出的曹老师摹写的皮耶罗·德·弗兰西斯卡的《传奇》，这是阿雷佐教堂的移步换景般建筑空间叙事不可分割的一部分。即便超越西方中心来看，世界性的远古洞穴壁画是不是也是如此呢。

绘画与其诞生和展示空间之间的关系发生了转变。这是第一个有趣的、关于绘画这种媒介背后某种整体性布局框架的转变。

媒介框架的另一个有趣的转变在于，这批绘画中大部分描绘的是其他绘画或艺术品，但不是这些作品本身，而是它们的存在：绘画和艺术品在它们被创造和展示的空间世界中。在这种游离进出的框架意识中，有时候曹老师干脆把注意力集中在他所观看的绘画作品的框架之间。

如果说曹老师的绘画是一面镜子，里面是其他镜子们，观照着观照们，这是伟大的画家中的画家维拉斯贵兹的《宫娥》的传统之一，是对媒介框架无穷无尽的转换和综合的极致追求。

!!

谈了这几点，我们实际上还在关于绘画媒介的某种意识形态框架上进出。我们只是揣测接近了曹老师绘画行为中的，较为整体的媒介意识框架，尚未接近其绘画本身，也就是那些色和料：包括那些不特别在意的话就不会发觉的，被曹老师赋彩为真实人物肤色的原单色大理石雕塑，以及其大量对早期文艺复兴绘画的形式和质量的援引等等。

!!

框架是空，空即是空，除了框架还是框架可以吗？

看起来曹老师是最不适合这个《讯息即媒介》研究系列的一集，但是似乎他其实也很善于玩弄媒介操控的，这让我们再次对旧媒介本身潜在的chatGPT能力展开遐思。

In the second episode of the "The Message is Medium" series, "Journey to the West, Cao Yingbin" seems to fully immerse us in the world of traditional medium—painting. Here, we attempt to select some points that can help us temporarily escape and change our viewing and thinking framework.

Hall 5 is a relatively enclosed, clean space, suitable for displaying small-scale paintings. Professor Cao believes that this exhibition hall is very "focused." Unlike the typical white box space, it is located in the narrow top floor of AMNUA Art Museum's spiral structure, resembling an elongated cave with a sloping ceiling.

!!

These paintings seem to oppose the concept of "easel" mobility and are considered to belong to this space and location naturally. This reminds us that, in the history of Western painting, before becoming "easel" paintings, paintings were largely attached to architecture and space as immovable "wall" paintings. A prime example is Piero della Francesca's "Legend," copied by Cao, which is an integral part of the changing architectural narrative of the San Lorenzo Church. Even when looking beyond the Western-centric perspective, could this also be true for ancient cave paintings worldwide?

The relationship between painting and its birth and exhibition space has changed. This is the first interesting shift in the overall framework of the medium of painting.

Another intriguing change in the media framework is that most of the paintings in this collection depict other paintings or works of art, not the works themselves, but their existence: the space in which they were created and displayed. In this floating framework awareness, Cao sometimes focuses his attention on the frames of the paintings he is observing. If we say that Cao's paintings are a mirror containing other mirrors, reflecting the act of reflection, this is part of the tradition of Diego Velázquez's "Las Meninas" and represents the ultimate pursuit of the endless transformation and integration of the media framework.

!!

Having discussed these points, we seem to have stayed in the discussion of the ideological framework of the painting medium without truly approaching the paintings themselves, such as the original monochrome marble sculptures given real human skin tones by Cao; and Cao's paintings, which also heavily reference early Renaissance paintings in form and quality.

The framework is empty, emptiness is emptiness, and nothing but the framework exists, right?

Cao seems to be the least suitable subject for this "The Message is Medium" research series. However, he seems to be adept at playing with media manipulation, prompting us to ponder once again the potential chatGPT capabilities of the old medium itself.

关于《西游记，曹应斌》

!**“破镜不重拾，落花难上枝” \_《讯息即媒介》第一季 第2集**西游记，曹应斌Journey to the West, Cao Yingbin"Shattered Mirrors Cannot be Reassembled, Fallen Flowers Cannot be Returned to the Branches"\_The Message is Medium  Episode 2

从柏拉图到曹应斌，在每个人身上，总是有一种自带的旧媒介与扑面而来的新媒介进行着斗争。对柏拉图而言，这是口语对话和传播文本的斗争；而对作为看起来十分传统的画家的曹应斌而言，这种可能并不外露和自知的对立又是什么呢？

亲身游历和绘画记录，柏拉图般的关于世界的“洞穴”框架的转换，一系列绘画操作中的，关于观看和表达的媒介的框架转换，这些共同组成了曹应斌《西游记，曹应斌》这一写生事件。接下来，最根本的问题是画家的媒介免疫问题，这指的是他们应对新媒介抵抗和接受抵抗的策略。

“抵抗性转换”是指画家看似拒绝或对新媒介漠不关心，但实际上新媒介已经掌握了画家，并成为他们处理世界的框架，即使他们自己可能并不承认。而“接受性抵抗”则是指，尽管他们看起来全盘接受了新媒介及其框架，实际上却将其当作旧媒介来使用。

From Plato to Cao Yingbin, there is always a struggle between the inherent old media and the oncoming new media in every individual. For Plato, this was the struggle between spoken dialogue and disseminated text; but for Cao Yingbin, who seems to be a very traditional painter, what is this possibly concealed and self-unconscious opposition?

Personal travel and painting records, the transformation of Plato's "cave" framework about the world, and the framework transformation of the medium in a series of painting operations regarding viewing and expression – these together form the sketching event of "Journey to the West, Cao Yingbin" by Cao Yingbin. Next, the most fundamental issue is the painter's medium immunity problem, which refers to their strategies for dealing with resistance to new media and acceptance of resistance.

"Resistance transformation" refers to painters seemingly rejecting or being indifferent to a new medium, but in reality, the new medium has already grasped the painters and become the framework for them to deal with the world, even if they themselves may not admit it. "Acceptance resistance," on the other hand, means that although they appear to fully accept new medium and its framework, they actually use it as if it were old medium.

\- chatGPTi, 23 04 02
