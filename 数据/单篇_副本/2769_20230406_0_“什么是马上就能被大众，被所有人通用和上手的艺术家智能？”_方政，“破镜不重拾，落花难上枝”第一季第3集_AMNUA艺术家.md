人人都能做艺术是人类的终极梦想。就在现在，什么是马上就能被大众，被所有通用和上手的“艺术家智能”？大众对新媒介充满了渴望。

《讯息即媒介》第三集是《艺术家：方政》，这一集从各个角度来看，似乎都与这个新媒介研究系列息息相关。

从前有一个地方叫做好莱坞，方政就在它所在的**加州洛杉矶**常驻。加州也是全世界的“硅谷”所在地之一，代表着“科技解放事业”的重要力量之一。恰逢此时，埃隆·马斯克正在将ChatGPT4的数据触角与推特分离，似乎要展开一场与ChatGPT的母公司OpenAI的竞争，关系到是否应该发展以及如何发展通用人工智能。这二个加州传奇有一个共同特点：它们看起来都是引领和创造 **通用人工梦想** 的地方，比如电影业和被设想为进入完全自由和充分发展时代的人类。对于后一个梦想，一个额外的好处是： **每个人都可以从事艺术创作**。

!

展览现场 exhibition view

然而，这种“通用”的人工智能还没有足够发展，就已经被资本和某种集体意志裹挟和操控了进程和方向。目前的实际情况是：并不是每个人都有能力，也被允许利用当前一系列最先进人工智能数字化创作技术。现实仍就是 **巨大的社会分工：** 传统的艺术家的智能VS通用人工智能？什么是 **马上就能被大家通用和上手的“艺术家智能”**？大众对此充满了渴望。

方政各个创作面貌的基础可能都建立在其基于数据积累、分析、重新处理和呈现的研究性项目上。在《艺术家：方政》中，《人造美人》和《对于全国美展的机器学习分析》可能主要是这两个研究项目的阶段性面貌。

!

展览现场 exhibition view

就当代艺术展览在某种程度上 **对观众的一般“预训练”** 模式而言，数字现实的信息被截断到当代艺术世界的展览现场，通过循环播放于液晶幕墙和被放大打印输出的墙纸前，观众可以选择匆匆走过或稍作停留，拍照上传分享，带来了新一轮的数据收割，数据驱动的艺术重新被融入 **数据现实** 中。

像《“墨镜啊魔镜，”\_林枞》中所提到的，大部分那些今天基于数据驱动的“数据绘画”和“数据雕塑”似乎并没有成功地在展览世界中再一次创造数据驱动的现实，无法真正驱动数字现实？

"Shattered Mirrors Cannot be Reassembled, Fallen Flowers Cannot be Returned to the Branches"\_The Message is Medium  Episode 3

The third episode of "The Information is Medium" is "Artist: Fang Zheng," and from various angles, it seems to be closely related to this new media research series.

There was once a place called Hollywood, where Fang Zheng resided in Los Angeles, California. California is also one of the locations of the world's "Silicon Valley," representing one of the important forces in the technology liberation industry. At this moment, Elon Musk is separating the data tentacles of ChatGPT4 from Twitter, seemingly launching a competition with OpenAI, the parent company of ChatGPT, which concerns whether and how to develop general artificial intelligence. These two California legends have a common characteristic: they both appear to be places that lead and create dreams of general artificial intelligence, such as the film industry and humanity imagined to enter a completely free and fully developed era. For the latter dream, an additional benefit is that anyone can engage in artistic creation.

However, this "general" artificial intelligence has been hijacked and manipulated by capital and some kind of collective will before it has developed sufficiently. The current reality is that not everyone has the ability, nor is allowed to use the current series of artificial intelligence digital creative technologies like Fang Zheng. The reality is a huge social division of labor: the intelligence of traditional artists versus general artificial intelligence. Right now, what is the general "artist intelligence" that everyone can use and master immediately? The public is eager for this.

The foundation of Fang Zheng's various creative appearances may be based on his research projects that are based on data accumulation, analysis, reprocessing, and presentation. In "Artist: Fang Zheng," "Artificial Beauty" and "National Art Exhibition Research" are possibly the phased appearances of these two research projects.

The information of these digital realities is materialized into the exhibition site of the contemporary art world. It can be viewed by audiences through loop playback on LCD screen walls and enlarged printed wallpapers, and they can choose to walk past hastily or pause briefly. As far as the "pre-training" of the audience in contemporary art exhibitions is concerned, this seems to be a bit like ChatGPT5.0 being rolled back to ChatGPT1.0. You might be able to cite a series of audience feedback actions of taking photos, uploading and sharing as a counterexample, but this response has not enhanced the effect of data art and its social intervention, or has only brought about a new round of data harvesting.

As mentioned in _"Mirror, Mirror,'\_Lin Cong's Readymade_,  today, the majority of data-driven "data painting" and "data sculpture" in the exhibition world seem to have failed to create a data-driven reality again, and cannot truly drive reality.
