**艺术家介绍**

!周一清 Zhou Yiqing

1952年生于江苏无锡1976年毕业于南京艺术学院美术系1989年毕业于南京艺术学院美术系油画研究生班现居南京，为南京艺术学院教授

1952 Born in Wuxi , Jiangsu Province1976 Graduated from School of Fine Arts, Nanjing University of the Arts with a Bachelor degree1989 Majored in oil painting, graduated from School of Fine Arts Nanjing,  University of the Arts with a Master degreeNow live and work in NanjingProfessor of School of Fine Arts Nanjing University of the Arts
