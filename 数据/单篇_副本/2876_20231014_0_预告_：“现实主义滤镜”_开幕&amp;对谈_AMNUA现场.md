南京艺术学院美术馆将于2023年10月15日下午两点在馆学术报告厅举办“现实主义滤镜”展览开幕式和对谈交流系列活动，敬请关注。

_AMNUA will hold the opening of the exhibition "Realism Filter" and a series of conversation and exchange activities on October 15, 2023 at 2:00 p.m. in the Academic Report Hall, please pay attention to it._

!

**论坛题目**：切入现实的探险

**与谈嘉宾** ：李怒、梁绍基、罗旖旎、余果、郑江

**主持人**：姚斯青

**时间** ：2023年10月15日   周日 14:00—15:00

**地点** ：南艺美术馆报告厅

本场论坛题为“切入现实的探险”，旨在挣脱将现实主义作为一种历史风格的陈旧理论叙述，在日益复杂且严峻的当下语境，将切入现实的批判性重新视为艺术实验的主题，探讨其中所面临的艰困和曲折，以及因之而生的艺术智慧。

论坛由独立学者、策展人姚斯青博士主持，邀请李怒、梁绍基、罗旖旎、余果、郑江这五位“现实主义滤镜”的参展艺术家，从他们各自的创作实践出发，共同探讨在今天艺术切入现实的诸种可能。

_Forum topic : The Adventure of Cutting into Reality_

_Guests with the talk : Li Nu, Liang Shaoji, Luo Yini, Yu Guo, Zheng Jiang_

_Moderator  :  Yao Siqing_

_Time  :   2023.10.15  14:00—15:00_

_Venue : AMNUA Lecture Hall_

_This forum, titled "The Adventure of Cutting into Reality", aims to break away from the stereotypical theoretical narrative of realism as a historical style, and in the increasingly complex and severe contemporary context, to reconsider the criticality of cutting into reality as the theme of artistic experimentation, and to explore the difficulties and twists and turns faced therein, as well as the artistic wisdom that has emerged as a result of it._

_Moderated by independent scholar and curator Dr. Yao Siqing, the forum invites the five participating artists of "Realism Filter", namely Li Nu, Liang Shaoji, Luo Yini, Yu Guo, and Zheng Jiang, to discuss the possibilities of art's penetration into the reality today from the perspective of their respective creative practices._

!

!

**论坛题目**：隔“身”投送的现实

**与谈嘉宾** ：石玩玩、盛剑峰、沈凌昊、姚聪

**主持人**：孟尧

**时间** ：2023年10月15日   周日 15:00—16:00

**地点** ：南艺美术馆报告厅

在万物互联的移动信息时代，我们生活的日常已经“无线”化与“投屏”化。生活、工作、娱乐的方方面面，都频繁在与各类电子屏幕的交互中达成。“在屏上”已然是一种新的日常现实。这样一种状况下，隔“身”投送不仅指向影像表达的媒介属性，也涉及被持续塑造的新生命链接关系。正因如此，讨论与身体相关的审美经验与创作可能，就是打开一种具体与当下的现实。

论坛由《画刊》杂志主编孟尧主持，邀请石玩玩、盛剑峰、沈凌昊、姚聪四位“现实主义滤镜”的参展艺术家进行讨论。

_Forum Topic :  The Reality of "Body" Delivery_

_Panelists : Shi wanwan, Sheng Jianfeng, Shen Linghao, Yao Cong_

_Moderator :  Meng Yao_

_Time :  2023.10.15  15:00—16:00_

_Venue : AMNUA Lecture Hall_

_In the mobile information era of the Internet of Everything, our daily life has become "wireless" and "screen casting". Every aspect of life, work and entertainment is frequently achieved through interaction with various types of electronic screens. "On screen" is already a new daily reality. In such a situation, the "body" projection not only points to the media property of image expression, but also involves the relationship of new life links that are continuously shaped. For this reason, discussing the aesthetic experience and creative possibilities related to the body is to open up a concrete and contemporary reality._

_The forum was moderated by Meng Yao, editor-in-chief of Art Monthly magazine, who invited four of the participating artists in "Realism Filter" - Shi Wanwan, Sheng Jianfeng, Shen Linghao, and Yao Cong - to have a discussion together._

!

**论坛题目**：作为时代滤镜的影像

**与谈嘉宾** ：陈文华、邬羽乔、夏天、许翔

**主持人**：陈伟

**时间** ：2023年10月15日   周日 16:00—17:00

**地点** ：南艺美术馆报告厅

在当代社会，数字影像充当了一种抽象的时代滤镜，重塑着我们对现实的抽象认知。它们不再仅仅是图像，而是表达、观点、情感的载体。每个像素、每一帧都是一个抽象的故事，一个抽象的世界。这些抽象滤镜通过引导和影响我们的审美体验，塑造着我们对当下文化和社会的抽象观点。数字时代的影像如同水面上的涟漪，传达着抽象的思想、情感和观点，成为我们对抽象现实主义的抽象反映。

本论坛由研究者、策展人、厦门大学创意与创新学院协作中心主任陈伟主持，邀请陈文华、邬羽乔、夏天、许翔四位“现实主义滤镜”参展艺术家进行讨论。

_Forum Title :  Images as Filters of the Times_

_Guests :  Chen Wenhua, Wu Yuqiao, Xia Tian, Xu Xiang_

_Moderator : Chen Wei_

_Time : 2023.10.15  16:00 - 17:00_

__Venue : AMNUA Lecture Hall__

_In contemporary society, digital images act as an abstract filter of the times, reshaping our abstract perception of reality. They are no longer just images, but carriers of expression, opinion, and emotion. Each pixel, each frame is an abstract story, an abstract world. These abstract filters shape our abstract views of current culture and society by guiding and influencing our aesthetic experience. Like ripples on the surface of water, images in the digital age convey abstract thoughts, emotions, and perspectives, becoming an abstract reflection of our abstract realism._

_This forum is moderated by Chen Wei, researcher, curator and director of Collaboration Center, Institute of Creativity and Innovation, Xiamen University, and invites the four participating artists of "Realism Filter" - Chen Wenhua, Wu Yuqiao, Summer, and Xu Xiang - to join the discussion._
