**展览前言**

**《“深美&深寻”——数智时代的艺术与新质生产力》**

1927年，刘海粟在上海美术专科学校（今南京艺术学院前身）面临办学危机时，提出“闳约深美”作为办学纲领，强调博采众长、精研深究、探索本质、追求至善的递进式教育路径，为困境中的学校指明了前进的方向。近百年之后，面对数智时代的机遇与挑战，当代南艺人积极应对新兴技术给教育、产业及社会结构带来的深远变革，以融合创新、服务社会为使命，构建具有中国特色、体现时代精神并具备全球视野的高水平综合性艺术类学科体系。“闳约深美”四字校训已然转换为南京艺术学院应对当今产业变革与技术迭代的不息变动。“闳”——指向数字时代的跨维度对话能力，“约”——坚守技术伦理的文化底线，“深”——探索人类认知的终极边界，“美”——重塑技术时代的艺术创造。

当下，一个全球性的认知革命正在悄然展开——“Deep Seek”（深度求索/深寻）不再仅仅作为技术路径或学术标签，而是成为中国智识界对数字文明核心命题的集体回应。在算法统治与元宇宙勃发的双重浪潮中，“深”从物理空间的垂直维度，蜕变为解码人类文明基因的密钥，更成为艺术领域重构创作伦理的哲学基石。

“深”之三重维度，可以理解为从技术深耕到文明解码的思辨过程：物理之深——数据海洋中的确定性追寻，“深”在此维度指向对“存在本质”的追问；文明之深——文化基因的算法觉醒，算法时代的模块化重组；精神之深——艺术创造的认知突围，探索“深”在艺术领域的本质。此次展览，正是以此三个维度为经纬，通过“物理之深”的技术确定性、“文明之深”的文化转码、“精神之深”的创作多元，构建“深美&深寻”的方法论闭环，进而发起一场关于“探索”与“抵达”的沉浸式对话。我们试图追问：何种“深”能使AI生成物超越“模仿”，成为真正的“艺术”？何为艺术之“深”？是技法传承的厚度，还是观念突破的锐度？在扁平化的平庸日常中，如何通过“深”的凝视，重新定义美学的求索与人类感知的边界？

当“深美”进入“深寻”时代，恰似一场微型文明实验——算法将生物特征转化为数字符号，再通过神经网络赋予其文化属性。这种“人机共创”的体验揭示了人工智能与艺术跨界在中国的终极愿景：不是技术的单向征服，而是文明基因的数字化转码与再生；不是艺术的终结，而是创作主权的分布式重构；不是全球化的文化趋同，而是“各美其美，美美与共”的数字诠释。在此语境下，展览提出了数智时代下的艺术创作理念：一是探寻数智时代的新质生产力。通过数字生成艺术打破“作者-观众”二元对立，重新诠释创作主权的“多极格局”。二是中国文化的传承与创新。从中华传统造物体系到智能制造，探寻技术伦理的文化根系，确保技术发展不离中国文化之“根”。三是算法语境下的再生性创造。以算法为笔，续写文明基因的无限可能，进而赋予人类文明演进以“永动机”。这三者共同构成此次展览的学术体系，在此次展览中，我们可以看到这些理念已初显出改变当下数字文明格局的潜力。

南京艺术学院苏州研究院致力投身于苏州人文经济学示范之城中，将艺术优势、区域资源与前沿创意、技术融合创新。在研究院启用伊始，借由展览之机，我们期盼南艺学子以“深”为舟，驶向数字文明的新大陆——那里没有绝对的技术霸权，只有文化基因与算法智慧的共舞；没有永恒的美学定式，只有持续深寻的诗意栖居。

“深美”不是目的，而是探索的姿态，让我们从“深”出发，重绘人类创造力的认知版图。

_**Foreword**_

Deep NUA & Deep seek - Art and New Productivity in the Age of Digital Intelligence

In 1927, Liu Haisu, when facing the crisis of Shanghai Fine Arts College (the predecessor of NANJING UNIVERSITY OF THE ARTS), put forward “Hong-Yue-Shen-Mei” as the program of running the school, emphasizing the progressive education path of learning from all the strengths, researching and studying, exploring the essence, and pursuing the best, which pointed out the direction of the school in the predicament. Nearly one hundred years later, facing the opportunities and challenges of the digital age, the contemporary Artists of NUA are actively responding to the far-reaching changes brought about by the emerging technologies to the education, industry and social structure, and with the mission of integrating innovation and serving the society, they are constructing a high-level comprehensive art disciplinary system with Chinese characteristics, embodying the spirit of the times and possessing a global vision. The motto of “Hong-Yue-Shen-Mei” has been transformed into a constant change of NUA to cope with today's industrial changes and technological iterations. “Hong” - pointing to the ability of cross-dimensional dialog in the digital era,“Yue” - adhering to the cultural bottom line of technological ethics. bottom line of technological ethics, “Shen/Deep”-exploring the ultimate boundaries of human cognition, and “Mei”-reinventing art in the age of technology. Beauty” - to reshape art creation in the age of technology.

Nowadays, a global cognitive revolution is quietly unfolding - “Deep Seek” is no longer just a technical path or an academic label, but has become the collective response of the Chinese intellectual community to the core proposition of digital civilization. In the dual waves of algorithmic domination and meta-universe explosion, “Deep” has transformed from the vertical dimension of physical space into a key to decode the genes of human civilization, and even become a philosophical cornerstone for reconstructing the ethics of creation in the field of art.

The three dimensions of “deep” can be understood as a discursive process from the deep cultivation of technology to the decoding of civilization: the depth of physics - the search for certainty in the ocean of data, in which “deep” points to the “essence of existence”. The depth of civilization - the algorithmic awakening of cultural genes, and modular restructuring in the age of algorithms; the depth of spirituality - the cognitive breakthrough of artistic creation, and the exploration of the role of “deep” in The depth of civilization - the algorithmic awakening of cultural genes, the modular reorganization in the age of algorithms; the depth of spirit - the cognitive breakthrough of artistic creation, exploring the essence of “depth” in the art field. This exhibition takes these three dimensions as the latitude and longitude, and through the technical certainty of “physical depth”, the cultural transcoding of “civilization depth”, and the creative plurality of “spiritual depth”, it constructs the “Deep NUA & Deep Seek”. The methodology of “Deep NUA & Deep Seek” is a closed loop, thus initiating an immersive dialog about “exploration” and “arrival”. We are trying to ask: what kind of “depth” can make AI-generated objects go beyond “imitation” and become real “art”? What is the “depth” of art? Is it the thickness of technique inheritance or the sharpness of conceptual breakthrough? How to redefine the search for aesthetics and the boundaries of human perception through “deep” gazing in the flattened and banal daily life?

When “Deep NUA” enters the era of “Deep Seek”, it is like a miniature civilization experiment - algorithms transform biological features into digital symbols, and then give them cultural attributes through neural networks. This “human-machine co-creation” experience reveals the ultimate vision of AI and art crossover in China: not the one-way conquest of technology, but the digital transcoding and regeneration of civilization genes; not the end of art, but the distributed reconfiguration of creative sovereignty; not the convergence of globalized cultures, but the “beauty of each”. Instead of the cultural convergence of globalization, it is the digital interpretation of “beauty for all”.

In this context, the exhibition puts forward the concept of art creation in the age of digital intelligence: first, to explore the new quality of productivity in the age of digital intelligence. Through the digital generation of art to break the “author-audience” dichotomy, reinterpretation of the “multi-polar pattern” of creative sovereignty. The second is the inheritance and innovation of Chinese culture. From the traditional Chinese creation system to intelligent manufacturing, we will explore the cultural roots of technological ethics to ensure that the development of technology does not deviate from the “roots” of Chinese culture. Third, regenerative creation in the context of algorithms. Using the algorithm as a pen, we will continue to write the infinite possibilities of civilization genes, and then give the evolution of human civilization a “perpetual motion machine”. These three together constitute the academic system of this exhibition, in which we can see that these concepts have shown the potential to change the current pattern of digital civilization.

The Suzhou Research Institute of NUA is committed to the Suzhou Model City of Humanities and Economics, integrating artistic strengths and regional resources with cutting-edge creativity and technology. At the beginning of the opening of the Institute, we hope that the students of NUA will take the “deep” as a boat and sail to the new continent of digital civilization, where there is no absolute technological hegemony, but only the dance between cultural genes and algorithmic wisdom; there is no eternal aesthetics, but only the poetic habitat of continuous deep searching. There is no eternal aesthetic stereotype, but only a poetic habitat of continuous deep search.

“Deep NUA” is not a goal, but a gesture of exploration. Let us start from ‘deep’ and redraw the cognitive map of human creativity.

2\. 中国文化之南艺文脉——数字艺术的“数据星云”：文化基因的算法觉醒

NUA Cultural Lineage — The "Data Nebula" of Digital Art: Algorithmic Awakening of Cultural Genes

!

蔡元培先生所提倡的“兼容并包”的办学理念，乃是主张学术自由与多元共存的跨文明观。在算法时代被转化为文化基因的模块化重组，从而创造出“东西方超现实景观”。这种“文化融合”的算法觉醒并非简单的风格拼贴，而是对文明基因的深层解码与再编码。本单元中的作品，从中华传统造物到智能制造体系，接续了“东海西海，心理攸同”的跨文明对话，进一步探寻数字时代中国传统文化基因的可迁移性与再生性。

!

The "inclusiveness" philosophy advocated by Mr. Cai Yuanpei emphasized academic freedom and pluralistic coexistence within a cross-civilizational perspective. In the algorithmic era, this concept has been transformed into modular recombination of cultural genes, thus creating "East-West hyperreal landscapes." This algorithmic awakening of cultural fusion transcends mere stylistic collage, representing a profound decoding and recoding of civilizational genes. The works in this unit, spanning from traditional Chinese craftsmanship to smart manufacturing systems, continue the cross-civilizational dialogue articulated in the axiom "The East and the West share the same psychology," while further exploring the migratory and regenerative potential of traditional Chinese cultural genes in the digital age.

3\. 深美精神之南艺回响——算法描绘的“文明基因”：艺术创造的认知突围

NUA Echo of the Profound Aesthetic Spirit — The "Civilizational Genes" Depicted by Algorithms: Cognitive Breakthrough in Artistic Creation

!

传统美学思想中的“技进乎道”，在当今的时代语境下，正逐步转化为文明基因的数字化转码与再生性创造。本单元中的作品，通过跨学科的技术融合，试图挑战“艺术创作权”的人类中心主义，打破“作者-观众”的二元对立。这是感官的视觉回应？亦或是人类精神新的可能性？当机械臂的雕刻精度超越人类大师；当AI生成的装置，通过拓扑优化呈现出后现代的解构美感，“技法传承”的厚度已无法完整定义艺术之“深”。此时的“深”，必须指向观念的锐度，进而以算法为笔，续写文明演进的无限可能。

!

The traditional aesthetic concept of "skill advancing toward the Dao" (jì jìn hū dào) is gradually transforming into the digital re-encoding and regenerative creation of civilizational genes in the contemporary context. The works in this unit, through interdisciplinary technical fusion, challenge anthropocentrism in artistic authorship and dismantle the author-audience binary. Is this a sensory-visual response? Or an unfolding potential of the human spirit? When robotic arms achieve sculptural precision surpassing human masters, and AI-generated installations employ topological optimization to present postmodern deconstructive aesthetics, the "thickness" of technical lineage can no longer fully define the "depth" of art. Here, "depth" must point toward the acuity of conceptual innovation, reimagining the algorithm as a brush to inscribe infinite possibilities for the evolution of civilization.

Suzhou Research Institute of NUA:

Located in the Yuanhetang National-Level Cultural Industry Demonstration Park, the institute covers a total floor area of 15,000 square meters. It plans to enroll approximately 200 postgraduate students in its inaugural phase and operates four innovation and research centers: Industrial Design, Digital Film & Television, Jewelry & Fashion Design, and Digital Intelligence Performance. With 13 innovation laboratories spanning Visual Culture Innovation Design, Handicraft & Fashion Wearable Design, Sustainable Human Settlements Design, Product System Innovation Design, Intelligent Interaction Innovation Design, Art & Technology Innovation, Digital Film Production, Animation Technology Creation, Music Wellness Research, Digital Intelligence Manufacturing, VR Performance for Pop Music, Digital Music & Video Production, and Panoramic Digital Music Development, the institute focuses on domains such as intelligent interaction, immersive media, sustainable design, and digital performance. It strives to build a comprehensive innovation chain integrating "technology R&D – artistic creation – industrial transformation."

Looking ahead, the institute will adhere to its developmental positioning of "rooting in Xiangcheng, serving Suzhou, connecting with Shanghai, and radiating the Yangtze River Delta." Guided by the philosophy of "art-driven, humanistic foundation, technology empowerment, and symbiotic integration," it aims to establish itself as a "practice ground" for cutting-edge humanistic economics, a "source of innovation" for cultural industry digitization, a "demonstration site" for government-industry-academia collaboration, a "new frontier" for interdisciplinary digital-intelligent convergence, and a "hub" for high-level digital-art talent cultivation. By advancing new-quality productive forces in the digital era and crafting a Suzhou model for humanistic economics, the institute will actively contribute to regional development and global cultural-technological innovation.
