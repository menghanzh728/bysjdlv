**《界面综合症》作品阐述**

界面综合症是一个长期项目，包括静态摄影和动态影像，主要的工作方式是将屏幕作为拍摄和研究对象，但前提是坏掉（异化）的电子设备屏幕。对屏幕显示的内容以及表面改造的再拍摄属于“后摄影”。Interface在英文语境中有界面、接口、接触的语义，而我们在网络时代人与人接触的主要媒介就是屏幕，屏幕已占据我们日常生活的大部分时间。这是后人类时代背景下人的异化，异化就是病变综合症（Syndrome）。

摄影的图像并非再现真实,而是某种虚构。我们一般情况下都在通过肉眼观看屏幕,但我们的肉眼已经逐渐被电子摄像镜头逐渐取代,我这个摄影实践是通过摄像镜头(电子眼)观看电子屏幕,即机器观看机器。以此讨论摄影的技术伦理——“观看”这一行为的主体与客体的模糊性。“摄影”这个媒介在抽离常规的被摄主体(人或景观)的介入会以何种面貌呈现？结果产生的是二手图像，还是三手图像？恰如当下泛滥的网络资讯轰炸

Channel-electronical biology 1-7

关于电子生物学 Electronic biology: 图像内容为当下的电子废弃品,底层的图表(已经过拆解与重组)和代码原来是机器的运行机制图,只要后台改写代码就会令到机器无法正常运行。在植入媒介、人机合体、基因改写的语境下,这些电子废弃品就好像人体器官(例如镜头=眼睛)。我很好奇人们以既有的伦理观如何看待它们、机器病变后会给人带来什么影响?

在指日可待的机器觉醒时代,作为客体的物件与作为主体的人将会以何种方式相处? 此系列图像内容的指涉还包括：数据隐私、机器进化、网络言论自由等相关议题，不一一赘述，更多解读的空间留给读者。

LCD Syndrome 界面综合症 Interface Syndrome #-6537

Photographicimage is a kind of fiction rather than reproducing reality. People watch the screen with naked eyes in general, but gradually the electronic camera has replaced human's eyes. This photographic practice is to watch the electronic screen through the camera lens (digital camera) which is similar to a machine watching another machine. We discuss the technical ethics of photograph through the practice‘the ambiguity of subject and object of ‘watching’. What kind of means will photography expose itself if it removes the conventional subjects (humans or Landscapes) Will it result in a second-hand image or a third-hand image? In the booming era of cyber information, human’s cognitive system is filled with the fragmentation of second-hand experience.

About Channel- electronical biology In the pictures are electronic wastes, and the bottom graphs (dissembled and recombined)and codes were showing the operational mechanism of the device, which cannot function properly once the backend codes are rewritten. Relating to medium planting,merging of human and machine as well as genereprogramming, these electronic wastesare like human organs (eg:cameras=eyes). I wonder how people viewthem with the existing morality, and what would happen if machines are diseased. Inthe future when machines wake up, how would article, as objects, get along withhumans, as subjects? The implications of this series of images include: data privacy,machine evolution, Internet freedom of expression and other related issues.
