**艺术家自述：**

作为一个艺术家，我把真实的精神世界寻找了一种恰当的抽象图像表达。语言抽象本身不是目的，是一种表达分享自己的审美判断视觉标准。从画面本身来讲，底层语言力量看似杂乱的理性布局与外面的圆相称以平衡，两种不同力量放在一起，构造了一个平衡的小宇宙，语言的简练适中，理性，创立一种空寂感。

“两种力量的对立，平衡”，能够引导自己创作，让作品趋于空灵，奔向自己内心所向往的豁达。作品中，运用平衡空静的概念，在创作中组合底层语言，笔迹的穿插，组合，形成节奏和情调；表层逆向画圆的力量感，本身就带有一种情调，笔迹的逆行痕迹，圆的形状，大小，位置用来平衡底层语言的情调，以求达到画面的平衡和谐。

好的艺术作品会不经意间触碰人的感官知觉，把人从理性和知识化的海洋里片刻的抽离，直达超验的境界。艺术需要天性的敏感神经跟后天性加持的逻辑性思考，作品只是一个精神传达的载体，自己在画面中运用简练语言以及画面空间留白处理，让作品趋于空灵安静，奔向自己内心所向往的豁达。

!

"The opposition and balance of the two forces" can guide me to create, to let the work tend to be ethereal, and rush to the open-mindedness that my heart is longing for. The concept of balance and quietness, the underlying language combined in the creation, interlaced and integrated calligraphy, form the rhythm and sentiment in the works. The powerful reverse circle itself has a sentiment. The retrograde trace of the handwriting, the shape, size and position of the round are used to balance the sentiment of the underlying language in order to achieve a harmonious state in paintings.

As an artist, I found a proper abstract image to represent a real spiritual world. Language abstraction itself is not the purpose, but a visual standard for expressing and sharing our own aesthetic judgments. From the perspective of the painting itself, the seemingly chaotic and rational layout of the underlying language power is balanced with the outer circle. Putting two different forces together and construct a small balanced universe. The language is concise, moderate and rational, which can create a sense of emptiness

Wonderful art works will inadvertently touch people's sensory perceptions, take people away from the ocean of rationality and knowledge, and then reach the realm of transcendence. Art needs inborn sensitivity and acquired logical thinking. Artwork is only a carrier of spiritual communication. Using concise language and white space to make artworks tend to be ethereal, and rush to the open-mindedness that you are longing for.
