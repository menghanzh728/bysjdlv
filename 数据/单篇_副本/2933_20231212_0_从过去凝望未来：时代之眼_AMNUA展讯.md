**本展览为江苏省文化和旅游厅2023年江苏省青年美术馆策展人扶持计划入选项目。**

南京艺术学院美术馆将于12月5日隆重推出《从过去凝望未来 \| 时代之眼》展览。本次展览以“视觉之思”为主要探讨内容，展出的作品汇聚了馆藏精品和当代新生代艺术家的作品，涵盖了水墨、油画、雕塑、装置、影像等多种艺术形式，呈现了过去与现在的艺术碰撞与交流。参展艺术家包括：范墨湖、金农、顾西梅、张大千、张善孖、程十发、罗尗子、叶浅予、周思聪、刘海粟、蔡斯民、曹志昊、冯且、方政、韩旭、梁昊鹏、刘成瑞、李颖、毛焰、乔相伟、施展、武艺、汪莺莺、汪琦琦、尹朝阳、张瑛、张斌、曾臻、翟宇辉。本次展览由青年策展人薛黑黑组织策划，并由南京艺术学院美术馆副馆长林书传担任学术主持。

展览展现了不同时期的艺术思想、风格和表达方式的碰撞。特别展示了南京艺术学院美术馆近现代的精品馆藏作品，这些经典作品见证了中华艺术的丰富历史，体现了传统艺术与当代创新的完美交融。除此之外，展览也展示了一批新生代艺术家的作品，他们以独特的视角、创新的思维和鲜明的个性，呈现了当代艺术的多元面貌。

本次展览提出了三个关于观看的延伸与分类：自然之眼、理念之眼以及机械之眼，主要探讨艺术家逐渐从主体对客体的描绘中脱离，以一种更为具体的视角切入现实生活或是以更为宏观的角度审视图像演变，最后在这个机械时代下，以视觉为切入点，重构人与物的关系，以及对新时代下的诸多新事物如：视频直播、电商环境、人工智能、元宇宙等的反思与展望。

展览将在南京艺术学院美术馆4展厅、0展厅以及影像厅展出，展览的第一部分将在12月5日南京艺术学院美术馆4展厅开始至2024年1月19日结束，第二部分将在12月19日南京艺术学院美术馆0展厅与影像厅开始至2024年1月19日。南京艺术学院美术馆邀请您共同探索“时代之眼”的独特魅力，感受传统与现代的奇妙交融。

On December 5th, the Art Museum of Nanjing Univeristy of Arts will launch the exhibition "Gazing into the Future from the Past \| Eyes of the Times". The exhibition will focus on "Visual Thinking", and will feature works from the museum's collection as well as works by contemporary artists, covering a wide range of art forms such as ink and wash, oil painting, sculpture, installation, video, and so on, presenting a collision and exchange of art between the past and the present. Participating artists include: Fan Mohu, Jin Nong, Gu Ximei, Zhang Daqian, Zhang Shanzhu, Cheng Shifa, Luo Qianzi, Ye Qianyu, Zhou Sicong, Liu Haisu, Cai Simin, Cao Zhi Hao, Feng and, Fang Zheng, Han Xu, Liang Haopeng, Liu Chengrui, Mao Yan, Qiao Xiangwei, Shi Zhan, Wu Yi, Wang Yingying, Wang Qiqi, Yin Chaoyang, Zhang Ying, Zhang Bin, Zeng Zhen, Zhai Yuhui. The exhibition is organized and curated by young curator Xue Heihei, and s academically hosted by Lin Shuchuan, deputy director of the Art Museum of Nanjing Univeristy of Arts.

The exhibition shows the collision of artistic ideas, styles and expressions in different periods. In particular, it showcases works from the modern and contemporary fine art collection of the Art Museum of Nanjing Univeristy of Arts. These classic works bear witness to the rich history of Chinese art and embody the perfect blend of traditional art and contemporary innovation. In addition, the exhibition also showcases the works of a group of new-generation artists, who, with their unique perspectives, innovative thinking, and distinctive personalities, present a diversified view of contemporary art.

The exhibition proposes three extensions and classifications of viewing: the natural eye, the conceptual eye and the mechanical eye, mainly exploring the artists' gradual detachment from the subject's portrayal of the object, to cut into the real life with a more specific perspective or to examine the evolution of images from a more macroscopic angle, and finally, in the mechanical era, to reconstruct the relationship between human and objects using the visual as an entry point, as well as to the many new things under the new era such as video broadcasting, e-commerce, and the new era. Finally, in this mechanical era, we use vision as a point to reconstruct the relationship between human and things, as well as to reflect on and look forward to many new things in the new era, such as live video broadcasting, e-commerce environment, artificial intelligence, and meta-universe.

The first part of the exhibition will be held from December 5, 2024 to January 19, 2024 in Hall 4 of the Art Museum of Nanjing Univeristy of Arts, and the second part will be held from December 19, 2024 to January 19, 2024 in Hall 0 and the Video Hall of the Art Museum of Nanjing Univeristy of Arts. AMNUA invites you to explore the unique charm of the "Times Vision" and experience the marvelous blend of tradition and modernity.
